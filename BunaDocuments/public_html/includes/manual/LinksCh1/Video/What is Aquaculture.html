Podcast: Harmful Algal Blooms

Harmful algal blooms, commonly referred to as red tides, are a big problem in our nation's coastal waterways and Great Lakes. As you can see here, toxic blooms of algae aren't always red! In our latest podcast, we interview a NOAA scientist to uncover what harmful algal blooms are, what they aren’t, and how we can learn to minimize their effects on our daily lives.
